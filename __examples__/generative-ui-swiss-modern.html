The Wall of Text Problem

Most LLM responses still collapse into text. Even strong models fall back to paragraphs and markdown.

  • Static, predefined interfaces limit AI capability
  • Text-only responses underuse model potential
  • Users forced to parse complex information manually
  • One-size-fits-all approach ignores context
